Background

Academic and Research Engagement

As a PhD student in Computer Science at the Florida Institute of Technology, I am specializing in Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare. My current research delves into cardiovascular disease (CVD), focusing on the application of advanced imaging techniques to enhance diagnosis and treatment. Recently, I have applied for an American Heart Association (AHA) proposal, which is centered on debiasing clinical care algorithms. This initiative reflects my commitment to developing AI solutions that are not only innovative but also equitable and ethical in healthcare.

Research Contributions and Industry Experience

With a strong foundation in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and healthcare applications, my academic journey includes publishing in esteemed international conferences, such as ICMLA, ICECET, etc. My industry experience spans roles as a software engineer, data engineer, and machine learning intern. These experiences have honed my skills in both research and practical applications, making me adept at addressing complex challenges in healthcare technology.

Determination for R&D

My ambition is to excel in research and development within the healthcare sector. Leveraging my background in software and data engineering, along with my current focus on AI in healthcare, I am committed to contributing to transformative solutions that advance medical technology and patient care.
